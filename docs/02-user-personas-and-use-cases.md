# User Personas and Use Cases

This document explains who Thrive AI is being built for, what real-life problems it should help with, and how different users might use the AI agent.

## Purpose of This Document

Thrive AI is designed to help real people, so the project needs to be built around realistic users and real problems. These personas and use cases help make sure the system is not just technically impressive, but genuinely useful.

The main goal is to understand the kinds of people who may need Thrive AI and the situations where the agent can make a meaningful difference.

## Persona 1: The Job Seeker

### Background

This user is looking for work but feels unsure where to begin. They may be a student, graduate, career changer, or someone who has been unemployed for a while.

### Main Problems

- Does not know how to organise a job search
- Feels overwhelmed by job applications
- Needs help improving a CV or cover letter
- Lacks confidence for interviews
- Struggles to stay consistent after rejections

### How Thrive AI Helps

Thrive AI can help the user create a job search plan, improve application materials, prepare for interviews, track applications, and stay motivated. The agent can also break the process into weekly tasks so the user always knows the next step.

### Example Use Case

A user tells Thrive AI, "I need a job, but I do not know where to start." Thrive AI asks about their skills, experience, preferred roles, location, and availability. It then creates a realistic job search plan with tasks such as updating the CV, finding job boards, applying to a set number of roles, and practising interview questions.

## Persona 2: The Student or Assignment Planner

### Background

This user is studying and needs help organising academic work. They may need support with essays, research projects, revision, or large assignments.

### Main Problems

- Struggles to start assignments
- Finds large tasks stressful
- Needs help creating outlines
- Wants guidance on research structure
- May leave work until the last minute
- Needs support without crossing academic integrity boundaries

### How Thrive AI Helps

Thrive AI can help users understand assignment requirements, create outlines, plan sections, manage deadlines, and organise research. It should support learning and planning rather than encouraging cheating or producing dishonest work.

### Example Use Case

A user says, "I need to write a 12-page assignment on why AI should be considered a threat despite its wide potential." Thrive AI helps create a title, thesis statement, section-by-section outline, research questions, argument structure, and writing schedule.

## Persona 3: The Confidence Builder

### Background

This user wants to improve confidence, social skills, communication, or self-belief. They may feel anxious in social situations, unsure how to speak to people, or worried about being judged.

### Main Problems

- Feels nervous when speaking to new people
- Avoids social situations
- Struggles with self-confidence
- Overthinks conversations
- Needs practice in a low-pressure environment
- Wants encouragement and realistic steps

### How Thrive AI Helps

Thrive AI can provide conversation practice, confidence-building exercises, reflection prompts, and small weekly challenges. It can help users prepare for real conversations, interviews, presentations, or social events.

### Example Use Case

A user says, "I want to be more confident when talking to people." Thrive AI asks what situations feel hardest, then creates a gradual practice plan. The plan may include short conversation starters, reflection exercises, role-play practice, and weekly confidence goals.

## Persona 4: The Health and Habit Builder

### Background

This user wants to build healthier habits, lose weight safely, improve routines, or become more consistent with exercise and nutrition.

### Main Problems

- Struggles with consistency
- Wants to lose weight but does not know how to plan
- Feels discouraged after setbacks
- Needs reminders and accountability
- May follow unrealistic or unsafe advice online
- Needs support that avoids medical diagnosis

### How Thrive AI Helps

Thrive AI can support general habit planning, meal reflection, activity tracking, routine building, and motivation. It must avoid giving medical advice, extreme dieting instructions, or unsafe health recommendations.

### Example Use Case

A user says, "I want to lose weight but I keep giving up." Thrive AI helps the user define a realistic goal, create simple weekly habits, track progress, and reflect on what makes consistency difficult. If the user mentions medical issues, the agent recommends speaking to a qualified professional.

## Persona 5: The Overwhelmed Goal Setter

### Background

This user has many things they want to improve but feels stuck. They may not have one clear problem; instead, they may feel generally unmotivated, disorganised, or unsure about their future.

### Main Problems

- Has many goals but no clear plan
- Feels overwhelmed by life decisions
- Starts things but does not finish them
- Needs help prioritising
- Wants encouragement without judgement
- Needs structure and accountability

### How Thrive AI Helps

Thrive AI can help this user identify their most important goal, break it into smaller steps, create a weekly plan, and review progress. The agent can help turn vague feelings into clear actions.

### Example Use Case

A user says, "I feel stuck and I do not know what to do with my life." Thrive AI responds with supportive questions, helps the user identify key areas of concern, and creates a first small action plan rather than overwhelming them with too much advice.

## Core User Needs

Across all personas, Thrive AI needs to support five core needs:

- **Clarity:** Help users understand what they want and what problem they are facing
- **Structure:** Break big challenges into smaller, manageable steps
- **Motivation:** Encourage users to keep going without making them feel judged
- **Accountability:** Track goals, tasks, and progress over time
- **Safety:** Know when to avoid risky advice and suggest professional support

## Main Use Case Categories

### 1. Goal Discovery

The user tells Thrive AI about a problem or goal. The agent asks focused questions to understand the situation and help the user turn a vague idea into a clear target.

Example:

"I want to improve my life, but I do not know where to start."

### 2. Action Planning

The agent creates a practical plan with steps, deadlines, and priorities.

Example:

"Create a four-week plan to help me apply for jobs while improving my confidence."

### 3. Progress Tracking

The user checks in with Thrive AI, marks tasks as complete, explains setbacks, and receives updated guidance.

Example:

"I only completed one task this week. Help me adjust my plan."

### 4. Coaching Conversation

The user uses Thrive AI for encouragement, reflection, role-play, or decision support.

Example:

"Can you help me practise answering interview questions?"

### 5. Academic Planning

The user asks for help understanding an assignment, building an outline, planning research, or managing deadlines.

Example:

"Help me plan a 12-page assignment on the risks of artificial intelligence."

### 6. Habit Support

The user receives help building routines around health, confidence, studying, or career progress.

Example:

"Help me build a morning routine that supports studying and fitness."

## Safety and Ethical Boundaries

Because Thrive AI deals with personal, academic, health, and emotional topics, it must include clear boundaries.

Thrive AI should:

- Support users without pretending to be a doctor, therapist, or legal expert
- Avoid extreme diet advice or unsafe health recommendations
- Encourage academic integrity and learning
- Suggest professional help when a user raises serious health or safety concerns
- Protect user privacy and avoid storing unnecessary personal data
- Give users control over what information is saved

## Early Product Requirement Summary

Based on these personas and use cases, the first version of Thrive AI should include:

- A goal intake flow
- A chat-based AI agent
- A way to choose support modes
- Personalized action plans
- Weekly tasks
- Progress tracking
- Basic memory for user goals and preferences
- Safety checks for sensitive topics
- Clear privacy and data controls

## Impact Statement

Thrive AI is being built to help people move from uncertainty to action. The project matters because many people do not fail because they lack potential; they struggle because they lack structure, confidence, support, or consistency. Thrive AI aims to give users a place where they can find their drive, organise their next steps, and begin to thrive in real life.
